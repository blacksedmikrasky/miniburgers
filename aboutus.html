We are a group integrated by Katya Monterroso, Ivonne Morales, Ian Herrarte, Sofia Cu, Juan Pablo Palma and Pablo Ibarra. We are seniors (in eleventh grade) from the science bachelor. This year, we present to to you our project for the event, consisting of a menu of mini burgers with the following ingredients:
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Flour
  • Ground Beef
  • Lettuce
  • Yellow cheese
  • Bacon
  • Butter

Step by step

  • Prepare the ingredients. Wash and chop all the cherry tomatoes, lettuce, bacon and cheese. Save for later.
  • Prepare the meat. In a bowl, mix the ground beef with one egg and some flour until it's merged. Make small beef patties and fry them with butter until well cooked.
  • Put some cheese on top of the patties and let it melt with the heat.
  • Fry the bacon and cut it in smaller pieces, same size as the rest of ingredients.
  • Connect all the ingredients with the toothpicks: cherry tomatoes, lettuce, beef patties with cheese and another tomato if you'd like. Decorate the toothpicks with stars if you want.
